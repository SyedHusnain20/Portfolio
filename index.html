01 — Capstone · Featured

قانون دان Qanoon Daan

A Pakistani Legal Advisory Chatbot powered by Retrieval-Augmented Generation. Embeds legal documents using sentence-transformers, retrieves relevant passages via FAISS, and generates grounded answers through Groq's Llama3-70b. Served via FastAPI with a Jinja2 frontend.
